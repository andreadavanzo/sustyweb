Sustainability Statement

We are aware that delivery to our customers is critically important, and sustainability is a part of that process. As such, we have calculated using this third-party product that our overall consignment weight for the year leads to 98,275Kg C02 due to the necessary use of freight shipping via air and road. To offset these emissions we have planted over 10,000 trees in a certified sustainable scheme which is in a preserved woodland thereby ensuring that the carbon will not be inaccurately accounted for.
